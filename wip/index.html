Paint blobs have invaded the canvas, but you have your shotgun.

Controls

  • When not aiming, use arrows to move
  • Ready your shotgun to start aiming space/gamepad button 1
  • When aiming, go towards the marked square up arrow/gamepad down arrow
  • When aiming, go back from the marked square down arrow/gamepad down arrow
  • When aiming, rotate clockwise right arrow/gamepad right arrow
  • When aiming, rotate counterclockwise left arrow/gamepad left arrow
  • Shoot your shotgun space/gamepad button 1
  • Reload your shotgun R/gamepad button 4

Reloading, shooting, and moving up or down consume a turn. Blobs will move after that, if a blob is close to you when it movesâ€¦ you are done.

Blobs will move first sideways and then up towards you. Blobs that collide with others while following you get trapped and can't move. Use walls and this knowledge to your advantage. Every 10 turns more and more blobs will be added.

You can customise the controls with the joystick-looking menu, and turn off the music with the note symbol.
