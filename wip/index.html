Shotgun Pollock

Paint blobs have invaded the canvas, but you got your shotgun.

Controls

  • Advance to the marked square up arrow/gamepad up arrow
  • Go back from the marked square down arrow/gamepad down arrow
  • Rotate clockwise right arrow/gamepad right arrow
  • Rotate counterclockwise left arrow/gamepad left arrow
  • Ready your shotgun space/gamepad button 1
  • Shoot your shotgun space/gamepad button 1
  • Reload your shotgun R/gamepad button 4
  • Stop music J/gamepad shoulder left
