Chord diagram of home/away goal difference for La Liga 2019-2020

This graph represents the goal difference (home goal match difference minus away match goal difference) for a season in La Liga.

The color of the chord is of the team that had the largest difference, and the size of the landing chord is the size of the difference.

You can click on the team short names or the sector markers to focus the view on that team.

The diagram is zoomable and panable.
